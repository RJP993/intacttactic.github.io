Betting

The Betting page is now up and running!

Bets are selected based on the JPScore probability compared to Bet365’s odds. I’ve always found team selections to be an area that betting markets can’t/don’t adapt well to, therefore this is the main influence on my outcome probability. Other factors such as home advantage, form, pace in attack and whether a team is offensive or defensive are taken into account to improve accuracy.

The bets are selected based on where the value is in the odds, NOT the mostly likely outcome. As a result the stake provided should be used as a guide to help maximise the long term return if you do decide on using the tips.

The Premier League and Bundesliga will be the only two leagues bet on for the first season. Both leagues have been tested on for about a year now and generally I’ve covered their teams more regularly than Spanish or Italian teams. If things go well another league will be added next season.

As much as anything else I like the idea of publicly putting the player ratings against the bookies to test their accuracy.

Anyway, hopefully it goes well. If you are going to use the betting section for tips I advise you read the disclaimer at the bottom of the site and accept that I won't always be right.

Players

The Players page will be changing quite drastically within the next few weeks. Instead of occasional write-ups on individuals, the Players page will instead contain a list of players with their JPScore rating over time and their predicted peak.

Originally, as mentioned in this post, the goal was to be a go-to source for people looking to see how good/bad a player is. Realistically this isn’t possible if each player needs a detailed write-up – there just isn’t enough time for it. There will still be occasional write-ups on players, however they will now be within the write-up section.

Approaching the player page this way allows me to cover more players, take requests, add more content and meet the initial goal. Similar to the betting page, I like the idea of putting my ratings out there to see how accurate I am over time. In general I just think this page will prove more interesting as a quick source for scouted players - especially the lesser known ones your club has just signed.

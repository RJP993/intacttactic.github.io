Ben Gibson - Analysis

England's best central defender.

25/08/2017

Copy Link

One of the unexpected occurrences of the 2016/17 Premier League season was the three breakthrough central defenders from the newly promoted sides. They were all English, under 24, confident defensively and comfortable in possession. At some point over the last 12 months each has received an England call-up and two of the three now play at top-half teams. The third is Ben Gibson, the best of the lot and the least likely to be selected for the 2018 World Cup squad.

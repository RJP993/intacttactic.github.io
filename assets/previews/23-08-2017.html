Dribbling without a plan.

With one year left on his contract and huge wage demands apparently preventing an extension, there’s a chance Oxlade-Chamberlain will leave Arsenal in the next week as rivals are reportedly willing to pay upward of £30m for him. The aim of this post is to explain why this would be a good thing for Arsenal.

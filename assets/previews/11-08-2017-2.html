Arsenal Season Preview 2017/18 - The 3-4-2-1
11/08/2017
Copy Link

After an up and down (but mostly down) 2016/17, Wenger unexpectedly decided on a tactical shuffle for the final six weeks. With 9 wins from 10 matches, including an FA Cup victory, the expectation is that Arsenal will remain in the 3-4-2-1 formation for the 2017/18 season.

There aren’t many teams harder to predict on a game-to-game basis than West Ham. Performances fluctuated so often last season that best player, Dimitri Payet, and fan favourite, Mark Noble, were targets of fan criticism just a year after they led the side to an impressive 2015/16 point total. Last season never really got started and by mid-January Payet had left. So, after two wildly different seasons, what’s next for West Ham?

Pedro Rodriguez - Analysis

Intelligence over flair.

16/08/2017

Copy Link

In eight years as a top flight player Pedro has won five league titles, including last year’s Premier League, been a starter in probably the greatest modern team for three of its four years, claimed two Champions League winners medals as a regular, and played a small role in Spain’s World Cup 2010 and Euro 2012 wins. Despite this, he’s never quite managed to convince fans of his quality. Why is that?

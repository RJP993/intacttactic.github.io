Dortmund - Squad Analysis
15/08/2017

Previewing Dortmund is quite a strange task. Predictions can be tricky given the number of youngsters, injury prone players and outcasts within the squad. Public opinion and JPScore ratings are further apart than with any other club. More uncertainties have been introduced by the managerial change, plus there is an overwhelming reluctance to criticise anything Dortmund do.

With no real title challenge since the league win in 2012, and only one season within 15 points of Bayern Munich in that time, what is going wrong at Borussia Dortmund?
